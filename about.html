Gabby Cresswell

Frances is created with longevity in mind, and often only producing quality one-off garments. Within her designer/maker framework, Cresswell is able to ensure quality through the artisan of each garment.

Frances garments are designed by Gabby in her farm-house workroom in Martinborough, New Zealand. Sometimes selected garments will be sewn in New Zealand by another skilled machinist, but mostly offshore in a factory in china.

The fabrics used in Frances garments are carefully chosen from New Zealand suppliers - prioritizing natural or regenerated fibres. Often vintage or found fabrics are re-purposed and saved from landfill.

Frances acts as a communication tool and this is reflected in the materials used and the changing silhouettes of each project. This methodology places a sustainable art practice at the centre of Frances, and this translates into structured, wearable and unexpectedly beautiful clothing.

Frances is currently based in Wellington, New Zealand.
